Overview

The Kedarkantha trek is one of the most stunning winter treks in India, offering breathtaking views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks. Starting from Sankri village, the trail weaves through dense pine and oak forests, frozen streams, and vast alpine meadows blanketed in white. The summit rewards trekkers with a panoramic view of peaks like Swargarohini, Bandarpoonch, and Black Peak.

This trek is perfect for beginners who want their first Himalayan summit experience, as well as seasoned trekkers looking for a scenic winter escape. Camping under star-lit skies at over 3,000 metres is an unforgettable experience.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1
Day
Dehradun → Sankri Village

Drive from Dehradun to Sankri (200 km, ~9 hrs). Check into camp, briefing session with guides, acclimatisation walk.

2
Day
Sankri → Juda Ka Talab (2,730 m)

Trek through dense forests of pine and oak. Arrive at the frozen lake campsite. Distance: 6 km, ~5 hrs.

3
Day
Juda Ka Talab → Kedarkantha Base (3,500 m)

Gradual ascent through open meadows. Stunning views of the Himalayan range. Distance: 4 km, ~4 hrs.

4
Day
Summit Day – Kedarkantha Peak (3,810 m)

Early morning summit push (3 hrs). 360° panoramic views. Descend to Hergaon campsite. Distance: 12 km.

5
Day
Hergaon → Sankri

Descend through pine forest back to Sankri. Final camp dinner and celebration.

6
Day
Sankri → Dehradun (Departure)

Morning drive back to Dehradun. Drop-off at railway station / bus stand by 6 PM.
